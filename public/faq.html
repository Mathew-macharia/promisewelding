Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our welding and fabrication services

What types of welding services do you offer?

+

We offer a comprehensive range of welding services including MIG welding, TIG welding, and arc welding. Our services cover gates, doors, windows, staircases, railings, and custom metalwork projects.

Do you provide free quotes?

+

Yes, we provide free consultations and quotes for all projects. Our team will assess your requirements and provide a detailed estimate before starting any work.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple projects like gates or grills might take 2-3 days, while custom staircases could take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline during consultation.

Do you offer custom designs?

+

Yes, we specialize in custom metalwork designs. We can work from your sketches, photos, or ideas to create unique pieces that match your vision and requirements.

What materials do you work with?

+

We work with various metals including mild steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and wrought iron. We can recommend the best material based on your project's requirements and budget.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

+

Yes, we stand behind our work with warranties. The specific warranty terms depend on the type of project and materials used. We'll discuss warranty details during the consultation.

Do you handle installation?

+

Yes, we provide complete installation services for all our products. Our experienced team ensures proper fitting and secure installation at your location.

What areas do you serve?

+

We serve the greater Nairobi area and surrounding regions. Contact us to confirm if we cover your specific location.
